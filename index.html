Dr. Ayesha Fatima Taimur

BDS, FAGE ( MHA)


Dr. Ayesha Fathima Taimur is a practicing doctor in India, who has also worked extensively in Egypt and Saudi Arabia. In the early years of her graduation, she was teaching Medicine and Dentistry in Rajiv Gandhi Dental College and Shyamala Reddy Dental College in Bangalore, India. She was teaching and superving junior doctors and medical students in the Dental College.

She has worked and been trained with Dr. Jeevan Aiyappa – a leading Implantalogist and Maxillo-facial Surgeon at Hosmat Dental Clinic from 1995 to 1999. Under his expert and professional guidance, she gained a lot of experience in learning and developing her skills to master Dentistry. She also assisted Dr. Jeevan in various head and neck surgeries involving accidents and trauma cases. She learned to work well as a team handling stressful situations and carry out specific procedures, e.g. performing operations and specialist investigations.

In 1999, she shifted to Saudi Arabia and worked at Al-Birk Government Hospital in Al-Birk, Mecca Region as a Dentist. She’s been working in the Saudi Arabia since 1999. As she is married to an Arab Doctor, She relates very well with the Arab culture and got familiarized with their needs and traditions. While interacting with different Arab patients, she got sensitized to their needs, their apprehensions, health concerns, their temperament and their distress over not getting the right kind of guidance relating to their health issues. She now travels back and forth from various Gulf countries and India. As the health care sector in India is affordable and of highest quality of specialized services with highly qualified and trained doctors, she decided to reach out to the patients of different nationalities seeking trusted medical care. Hence she entered the health care sector as a Medical Facilitator both in India and abroad. Her amiability of temperament and honesty in the assigned job are some of the commendable traits of her personality that endeared her amongst various Arabs.

She is well versed with the Arabic language and can comprehend various dialects of the region. She is also fluent in English, Urdu and various Indian languages.

She has the ability to work well within a team, handle multiple tasks and identify and swiftly solve problems. She is friendly and energetic with strong communication and interpersonal skills.

Her Routine includes contacting patients of different nationalities who require various medical treatment in Bangalore India, She then identifies and analyzes the best hospital available for a particular ailment. she communicates with the specialized doctors who are the best in that required specialty. The patient will have freedom and flexiblity to finalize the hospital they desire and choose their time of travel to India. She will work with other doctors as part of a team, either in the same department or within other specialties, coordinate with other medical and non-medical staff in the hospital to ensure quality treatment. Her work involves various intellectually challenging factors that play a role in determining the ultimate success. She possesses the ability to analyze situations and focus on the essentials quickly.

Doctor Ayesha personally will be in touch with the patient and their family from the time of arrival to India until they return satisfied to their countries
